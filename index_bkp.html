❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting your website built with Onboard Digital.

How much does a website cost in Pune? +
Our websites start at just ₹4,999 for small businesses. The Business package is ₹9,999 and includes SEO content and Google Search Console setup. Our Premium package at ₹19,999 includes AI chatbot and Google Ads. All prices are one-time with no hidden fees.
How long does it take to build my website? +
Most websites are delivered within 3 to 7 working days from the time you share your content and approve the design. Premium projects with more pages may take up to 10 days.
Do I need to know coding or technology? +
Not at all! We handle everything — design, development, hosting, SEO setup, and even Google Search Console. You just share your business details and we take care of the rest. We communicate entirely over WhatsApp.
Will my website appear on Google? +
Yes! All our websites are built with SEO best practices — proper title tags, meta descriptions, fast loading speeds, and mobile responsiveness. Business and Premium packages include Google Search Console submission so Google indexes your site faster.
Which cities do you serve? +
We are based in Kharadi, Pune and primarily serve clients in Pune, Hyderabad, and Visakhapatnam (Vizag). However, since all work is done online via WhatsApp, we work with businesses across India.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash for local clients in Pune. We typically ask for 50% advance and 50% on delivery.
